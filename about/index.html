About ABWA Alumni Association
Info:
Launched by the first batch graduating out of ABWA IBDP, the batch of 2012, the Alumni Association currently comprises of 3 batches and consists of 75 students. In the coming years, as the school adds the A-level system, there will be more students joining the association. The council comprises of a President and 6 members, with atleast one representative from every batch to help with the planning and organizing of events as well as setting the path for a larger association and council in the coming years.
